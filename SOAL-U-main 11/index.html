Summer of a Lifetime

A Leadership Experience for the Next Generation

SOAL U is an immersive summer program in Princeton designed for high school students who want to develop the skills, confidence, and intellectual curiosity required to lead in the modern world.

Through exposure to STEAM disciplines, writing, leadership development, and collaborative learning, students experience an environment modeled on the culture of great universities. Students are challenged to think deeply, communicate clearly, and solve complex problems.

THE SOAL U @ Princeton DIFFERENCE
