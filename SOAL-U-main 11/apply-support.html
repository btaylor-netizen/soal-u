FOUNDER

Brian Taylor

  • Princeton University
  • Professional Basketball Champion
  • Educator and Youth Advocate

Brian Taylor created SOAL U to provide students with the type of transformative educational exposure that shaped his own life journey—from the projects of Perth Amboy to Princeton University and beyond.

SOAL U reflects his belief that exposure to great ideas and environments can change the trajectory of a young person's life.
